Hello! I am an Event Manager and Web Developer from Indianapolis, Indiana. I graduated from Ball State University's Miller School of Business in 2018 with a Bachelor's Degree in Hospitality Management with a Minor in Theatre. After school I became the Event Manager for the Indianapolis Symphony Orchestra where I managed events with crowds up to 13,000. Due to the pandemic the Symphony was required to close until further notice and a majority of the organization was furloughed. During this time I decided to grow my skills and sign up for Eleven Fifty Academy's 12 week immersive bootcamp for Web Development. Through this course, I learned different coding languages such as, HTML, CSS and Javascript. I was able to apply the skills I learned to real world projects and work with my classmates in team settings. Now that I am finished with this course, I am continuing my studies through self-taught classes and looking for a career that can use both my event management skills and my new coding skills.
